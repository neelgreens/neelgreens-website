About NeelGreens LLP

NeelGreens LLP is a visionary initiative aimed at promoting the rich culture, pristine nature, and diverse activities of the Nilgiris district. Established with a mission to celebrate and preserve the natural beauty and heritage of the region, NeelGreens LLP operates from its base in Kotagiri. The company’s flagship project, NeelGreens Farm, is located in the serene Konavakorai village in Kotagiri, where organic farming and eco-friendly practices take center stage.

Our Vision

To create a harmonious blend of sustainable development and cultural preservation, fostering a deeper connection between people and nature while supporting the local community.

Our Mission

  • To promote the Nilgiris district’s cultural and natural heritage through innovative and sustainable activities.
  • To offer enriching experiences like yoga and meditation retreats, treks, and adventure activities that bring people closer to nature.
  • To cultivate and market premium organic produce under the NeelGreens brand, supporting health and wellness worldwide.

Our Founders

  • Praveen Agarwal, Hyderabad - Managing Director of Meraqee.
  • Satyarup Siddhanta, Kolkata - Guinness World Record holder, renowned mountaineer, and Founder and Director of Winners and Achievers.

What We Offer

  • Experiential Retreats: Yoga and meditation retreats to rejuvenate the mind, body, and soul.
  • Adventure and Nature Activities: Exciting treks and nature-based adventures that explore the heart of the Nilgiris.
  • Organic Produce: Premium organic honey, exotic teas, and other local produce from the Nilgiris, cultivated with care at NeelGreens Farm.

Sustainability and Community Focus

At NeelGreens, we believe in sustainable living and community upliftment. By promoting organic farming and partnering with local farmers, we ensure eco-friendly practices and fair trade. Our activities not only showcase the region’s beauty but also empower the local community, preserving traditions and enhancing livelihoods.
